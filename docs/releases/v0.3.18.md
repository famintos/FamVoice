### Changed

- Recording now starts closer to the moment you press the hotkey, so the beginning of your speech is captured more reliably after idle.
- The microphone input is preloaded when the app starts or when you switch devices, which reduces wake-up overhead on the next recording.

### Fixed

- Fixed a latency regression introduced after the microphone stream began releasing between recordings on Windows.
- Fixed early speech being more likely to get clipped right after pressing the hotkey while keeping the microphone released when FamVoice is idle.
