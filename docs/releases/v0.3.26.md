### Fixed

- Stopped FamVoice from leaving dictated text in your clipboard when clipboard copy is turned off. Auto-paste now restores your previous clipboard contents instead of replacing them permanently.
- Made clipboard copy work independently from auto-paste. When clipboard copy is turned on, the final transcript is saved to your clipboard as soon as you finish speaking, even if nothing is pasted.

### Changed

- Renamed the clipboard setting in Settings so it reflects the actual behavior: copying the finished transcript to your clipboard after dictation.
- Kept history re-paste isolated from the new transcript clipboard setting so re-pasting old items still restores your previous clipboard contents afterward.
